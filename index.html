About Myself

Background

I'm a Portland native, born and raised here in the northwest. I'm a husband to a wonderful woman and proud father of three children.

For the last four years, I worked as a wafer fab operator for a semiconductor company in the Portland metro area. I was unable to find a career job in my field of study, thus led me to reassess what I wanted out of a career and what I wanted to do in the future.

Some of my good friends from highschool went on to pursue education in computer science in college. They all work in the tech industry and have their career jobs. One of my friends also runs a small marketing company with his brother, where developing websites is one of the services they provide for their clients. This provided me with a new outlook on what I wanted from a professional career, so I made the decision to go with web developing. Hopefully my skills and background in design will aid me in my pursuit of this goal.

Education

I attended Benson Polytechnic High School in Portland where I majored in computer science, which may have been my first foray into a code based environment. I then studied Architecture and graduated from Portland State University in 2010.

Hobbies

I like living in Oregon and I wouldn't have it any different. I enjoy hiking in the gorge and fishing in Oregon's bountiful lakes, rivers, and streams. I'm an avid car enthusiast, part of a local car club and race gokarts with my fellow gearheads. I'm a natural artist, but lack formal art education and technique. I make up for this with the ability to translate ideas visually with pencil and paper. Photography is also something that I enjoy doing on my free time, but I'm probably amateur at best.
