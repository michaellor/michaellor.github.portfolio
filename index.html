About Myself

I'm a Portland native, born and raised here in the northwest. I attended Benson Polytechnic High School in Portland where I majored in computer science, which have been my first foray into a code based environment. I then studied Architecture and graduated from Portland State University in 2010.

I'm a husband to a wonderful woman and proud father of three children.

I like living in Oregon and I wouldn't have it any different. I enjoy hiking in the gorge and fishing for trout in the summer. I'm an avid car enthusiast, part of a local car club and race gokarts with my fellow gearheads. I'm a natural artist, but lack formal art education and technique. I make up for this with the ability to translate ideas visually with pencil and paper.
